About Us

At MyExpoBiz, we connect buyers with Myanmar’s finest agricultural produce through a strong, trusted sourcing network built over more than 10 years of hands-on trading experience in the local market.

We actively procure pulses, beans, maize, and rice from across Myanmar—working with a wide base of regional suppliers who source directly from farmers—and supply to major buyers, processors, and wholesalers in Yangon and other key trading hubs. This deep-rooted local presence allows us to secure consistent quality, competitive pricing, and reliable volumes throughout the year.

At the same time, we support buyers in both local and international markets through professional logistics and documentation partners, ensuring smooth delivery, compliant documentation, and timely shipments according to agreed terms. Our team manages the full sourcing and fulfillment process, from origin procurement to final dispatch.

Our operations are flexible and buyer-oriented. We arrange product processing according to customer requirements—such as cleaning, grading, sizing, packing, and moisture control—before delivery. With access to hundreds of suppliers from rural producing areas, we have the capacity to source large volumes within tight timelines and fulfill orders before agreed delivery dates.

Whether you are a domestic wholesaler, processor, or an international importer, MyExpoBiz aims to be your reliable sourcing partner in Myanmar—bringing you consistent supply, flexible terms, and responsive service.
